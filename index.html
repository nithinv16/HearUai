About HearUAI

More Than Just an AI - Your Emotional Companion

HearUAI represents a breakthrough in AI therapy and companionship. Unlike traditional chatbots, HearUAI combines the clinical expertise of a licensed therapist with the emotional warmth of a trusted friend.

Our AI doesn't just respond to your words - it understands your emotions, remembers your journey, and adapts its personality to what you need most. Whether you're seeking structured therapy or just someone to talk to about your day, HearUAI is there with the perfect response, tone, and energy.

Dual-Mode Intelligence: Seamlessly switches between professional therapy sessions and friendly conversations
Long-term Emotional Memory: Remembers your triggers, preferences, and progress over time
Gender-Adaptive Personality: Adjusts communication style based on your preferences and mood
Real-time Mood Detection: Analyzes your emotional state and responds appropriately
